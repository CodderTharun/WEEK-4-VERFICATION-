## Synchronous Machines

### Generators
- Constant speed operation
- Connected to grid
- Frequency = 50/60 Hz

### Motors
- Constant speed independent of load
- Used for constant speed applications

### Equivalent Circuit
- Synchronous reactance Xs
- Back EMF Eb

## Induction Motors

### Squirrel Cage
- Most common
- Rugged and simple
- Slip-dependent speed

### Wound Rotor
- Speed control via rotor resistance

### Operating Principle
- Rotating magnetic field
- Induced currents in rotor
- Torque production
