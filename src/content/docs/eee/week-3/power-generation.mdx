## Geothermal Energy

### Advantages
- Constant output (baseload)
- High efficiency (10-15%)
- Low environmental impact

### Disadvantages
- Limited locations
- High exploration risk

### Types
- Dry steam
- Flash steam
- Binary cycle

## Biomass Energy

### Sources
- Wood, agricultural waste
- Landfill gas
- Biodiesel

### Conversion
- Direct combustion
- Gasification
- Fermentation

### Environmental Impact
- Carbon neutral (renewable carbon)
- Efficiency: 15-40%
