## Transient Response

### RC Circuit
- τ = RC
- Charging/discharging time constant

### RL Circuit
- τ = L/R
- Rise time and settling time

### RLC Circuit
- Underdamped, critically damped, overdamped
- Natural frequency and damping ratio

## Resonance

### Series RLC
- Resonant frequency: ω₀ = 1/√(LC)
- Quality factor: Q = ω₀L/R = 1/(ω₀CR)

### Parallel RLC
- Similar but different Q definition

- Filtering
- Tuning circuits
