## Three-Phase Power

### Balanced System
- VAB, VBC, VCA phase sequence
- 120° phase angles

### Y Connection
- VL = √3 × VP
- IL = IP

### Δ Connection
- VL = VP
- IL = √3 × IP

## Power Flow

### Real and Reactive Power
- P = √3 VL IL cos φ
- Q = √3 VL IL sin φ

## Load Types

- Resistive loads
- Inductive loads
- Capacitive loads
- Motor loads
