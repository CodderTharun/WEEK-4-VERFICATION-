## AC Fundamentals

### Sinusoidal Signals
- v(t) = Vm sin(ωt + φ)
- RMS voltage = Vm/√2

### Phasors
- Complex representation
- V = V∠φ = V(cos φ + j sin φ)

## Impedance

### Resistor: Z = R
### Inductor: Z = jωL
### Capacitor: Z = -j/(ωC)

## AC Power

### Real Power (P)
- P = VI cos φ (Watts)

### Reactive Power (Q)
- Q = VI sin φ (VAR)

### Apparent Power (S)
- S = VI (VA)
- S² = P² + Q²
