## Thermal Power

### Coal Power Plants
- Combustion → Steam → Turbine
- Efficiency: 35-42%
- High pollution

### Natural Gas
- Gas turbine power plants
- Combined cycle plants
- Efficiency: 40-60%

## Hydroelectric Power

### Advantages
- Renewable
- Long life (50-100 years)
- Low operating cost

### Disadvantages
- Environmental impact
- High capital cost
- Limited locations

## Nuclear Power

- Fission process
- High efficiency (33%)
- Safety concerns
