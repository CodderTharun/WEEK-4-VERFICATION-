# Electrical Machines - Week 1

## Machine Classification

### DC Machines
- Brushes and commutator
- Armature and field winding

### AC Machines
- Synchronous machines
- Induction machines

## Basic Principles

### Faraday's Law
- EMF = -dΦ/dt

### Lorentz Force
- F = B × I × L

### Motor Principle
- Torque from magnetic field and current

### Generator Principle
- EMF from relative motion
