## Power System Structure

### Generation
- Power plants (11-25 kV)
- Thermal, hydro, nuclear, renewable

### Transmission
- High voltage (100-765 kV)
- Long distance, low losses
- Step-up and step-down transformers

### Distribution
- Primary (50-230 kV)
- Secondary (11-34.5 kV)
- Low voltage (120-480 V)

## Key Components

- Generators
- Transformers
- Transmission lines
- Distribution lines
- Switches and breakers
- Load centers
