## Basic Elements

### Resistor
- V = I × R (Ohm's Law)
- Power: P = I²R = V²/R

### Inductor
- V = L × dI/dt
- Energy stored: W = ½LI²

### Capacitor
- I = C × dV/dt
- Energy stored: W = ½CV²

## Circuit Laws

### Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL)
- Sum of voltages in loop = 0

### Kirchhoff's Current Law (KCL)
- Sum of currents at node = 0

### Ohm's Law
- V = I × R
