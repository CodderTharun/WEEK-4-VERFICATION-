## Question 1

**x is a positive integer such that x² + 12 is exactly divisible by x. Find all the possible values of x.**

A) 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12  
B) 1, 2, 4, 8, 12  
C) 2, 3, 5, 6, 12  
D) 1, 3, 6, 9, 12  

**Correct Answer: A) 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12**  
**Explanation:**   
Given: (x² + 12) is exactly divisible by x. ⇒ (x² + 12) / x is an integer ⇒ x²/x + 12/x is an integer ⇒ x + 12/x is an integer.   
Since x is already an integer, 12/x must also be an integer.   
Therefore, x must be a divisor of 12.   
The positive divisors of 12 are: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.    
Hence, the possible values of x are: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.

## Question 2

**Find the unit's digit in the product (2467)¹⁵³ × (341)⁷².**

A) 1  
B) 3  
C) 7  
D) 9  

**Correct Answer: C) 7**  
**Explanation:**   
The unit's digit in the given product depends only on the unit digits of the numbers.   
So, we consider: 7¹⁵³ × 1⁷².    
7⁴ gives unit digit 1, therefore 7¹⁵² gives unit digit 1, hence 7¹⁵³ gives unit digit 1 × 7 = 7.    
Also, 1⁷² gives unit digit 1.    
Therefore, the unit digit in the product is: 7 × 1 = 7.

## Question 3

**Simplify: (789×789×789 + 211×211×211) / (789×789 – 789×211 + 211×211)**

A) 211  
B) 578  
C) 1000  
D) 789  

**Correct Answer: C) 1000**  
**Explanation:**  
Given expression: (789³ + 211³) / (789² – 789×211 + 211²).    
Using the identity: (a³ + b³) / (a² – ab + b²) = a + b.    
Here, a = 789 and b = 211.   
Therefore: 789 + 211 = 1000.

## Question 5

**Find the H.C.F. and L.C.M. of the fractions: 2/3, 8/9, 16/81 and 10/27**

A) H.C.F. = 2/81, L.C.M. = 80  
B) H.C.F. = 2/81, L.C.M. = 80/3  
C) H.C.F. = 2/27, L.C.M. = 80/81  
D) H.C.F. = 1/81, L.C.M. = 80/27  

**Correct Answer: B) H.C.F. = 2/81, L.C.M. = 80/3**    
**Explanation:**   
H.C.F. of fractions = (H.C.F. of numerators) / (L.C.M. of denominators).   
L.C.M. of fractions = (L.C.M. of numerators) / (H.C.F. of denominators).    
H.C.F. of numerators (2, 8, 16, 10) = 2. L.C.M. of denominators (3, 9, 81, 27) = 81.    
Therefore H.C.F. = 2/81.   
L.C.M. of numerators (2, 8, 16, 10) = 80.    
H.C.F. of denominators (3, 9, 81, 27) = 3. Therefore L.C.M. = 80/3.

## Question 9

**A sum of ₹427 is divided among A, B and C such that 3 times A's share, 4 times B's share and 7 times C's share are equal. Find the share of each.**

A) ₹196, ₹147, ₹84  
B) ₹180, ₹147, ₹100  
C) ₹200, ₹140, ₹87

A's share = (28/61) × 427 = ₹196.    
B's share = (21/61) × 427 = ₹147.    
C's share = (12/61) × 427 = ₹84.

A) 3%  
B) 4%  
C) 5%  
D) 6%  

**Correct Answer: D) 6%**  
**Explanation:**    
Principal (P) = ₹12,500.

Using SI = (P × R × T) / 100: 3000 = (12500 × R × 4) / 100 ⇒ 3000 = 500R ⇒ R = 6%.

**A quasi-static process is:**

A) Very fast  
B) Always irreversible

## Question 58

**The work ratio of a gas turbine plant is given by:**

A) Net work output / Work done by the turbine  
B) Work done by the turbine / Net work output  
C) Net work output / Heat supplied  
D) Heat supplied / Net work output  

**Correct Answer: A) Net work output / Work done by the turbine**  
**Explanation:**   
The work ratio = Net work output / Work done by the turbine.           
It represents how much of the turbine's gross output is available as net output after accounting for compressor work.
