## Question 1

1. The speed of a boat when travelling downstream is 32 km/hr, whereas when travelling upstream it is 28 km/hr. What is the speed of the boat in still water and the speed of the stream?

- A) 30 km/hr, 2 km/hr
- B) 28 km/hr, 4 km/hr
- C) 32 km/hr, 2 km/hr
- D) 30 km/hr, 4 km/hr

Correct Answer: A) 30 km/hr, 2 km/hr

Explanation:

Downstream speed = 32 km/hr

Upstream speed = 28 km/hr

Speed of boat in still water

= (32 + 28) / 2

= 60 / 2

= 30 km/hr

Speed of stream

= (32 − 28) / 2

= 4 / 2

= 2 km/hr

## Question 14

14. The value of k for which the quadratic equation kx(x − 2) + 6 = 0 has equal roots is:

- A) 2
- B) 3
- C) 6
- D) −6

Correct Answer: C) 6

Explanation:

Given equation

kx(x − 2) + 6 = 0

⇒ kx² − 2kx + 6 = 0

Comparing with ax² + bx + c = 0,

a = k

b = −2k

c = 6

For equal roots,

b² − 4ac = 0

⇒ (−2k)² − 4(k)(6) = 0

⇒ 4k² − 24k = 0

⇒ 4k(k − 6) = 0

⇒ k = 0 or k = 6

Since k ≠ 0 for a quadratic equation,

k = 6
