## Question 1

**x is a positive integer such that x² + 12 is exactly divisible by x. Find all the possible values of x.**

A) 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12  
B) 1, 2, 4, 8, 12  
C) 2, 3, 5, 6, 12  
D) 1, 3, 6, 9, 12  

**Correct Answer: A) 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12**  
**Explanation:**  
Given: (x² + 12) is exactly divisible by x. ⇒ (x² + 12) / x is an integer ⇒ x²/x + 12/x is an integer ⇒ x + 12/x is an integer.  
Since x is already an integer, 12/x must also be an integer.  
Therefore, x must be a divisor of 12.  
The positive divisors of 12 are: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.  
Hence, the possible values of x are: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.

## Question 2

**Find the unit's digit in the product (2467)¹⁵³ × (341)⁷².**

A) 1  
B) 3  
C) 7  
D) 9  

**Correct Answer: C) 7**  
**Explanation:**  
The unit's digit in the given product depends only on the unit digits of the numbers.  
So, we consider: 7¹⁵³ × 1⁷².  
7⁴ gives unit digit 1, therefore 7¹⁵² gives unit digit 1, hence 7¹⁵³ gives unit digit 1 × 7 = 7.  
Also, 1⁷² gives unit digit 1.  
Therefore, the unit digit in the product is: 7 × 1 = 7.

## Question 3

**Simplify: (789×789×789 + 211×211×211) / (789×789 – 789×211 + 211×211)**

A) 211  
B) 578  
C) 1000  
D) 789  

**Correct Answer: C) 1000**  
**Explanation:**  
Given expression: (789³ + 211³) / (789² – 789×211 + 211²).  
Using the identity: (a³ + b³) / (a² – ab + b²) = a + b.  
Here, a = 789 and b = 211.  
Therefore: 789 + 211 = 1000.

## Question 5

**Find the H.C.F. and L.C.M. of the fractions: 2/3, 8/9, 16/81 and 10/27**

A) H.C.F. = 2/81, L.C.M. = 80  
B) H.C.F. = 2/81, L.C.M. = 80/3  
C) H.C.F. = 2/27, L.C.M. = 80/81  
D) H.C.F. = 1/81, L.C.M. = 80/27  

**Correct Answer: B) H.C.F. = 2/81, L.C.M. = 80/3**  
**Explanation:**  
H.C.F. of fractions = (H.C.F. of numerators) / (L.C.M. of denominators).  
L.C.M. of fractions = (L.C.M. of numerators) / (H.C.F. of denominators).  
H.C.F. of numerators (2, 8, 16, 10) = 2. L.C.M. of denominators (3, 9, 81, 27) = 81.  
Therefore H.C.F. = 2/81.  
L.C.M. of numerators (2, 8, 16, 10) = 80. H.C.F. of denominators (3, 9, 81, 27) = 3. Therefore L.C.M. = 80/3.

## Question 9

**A sum of ₹427 is divided among A, B and C such that 3 times A's share, 4 times B's share and 7 times C's share are equal. Find the share of each.**

A) ₹196, ₹147, ₹84  
B) ₹180, ₹147, ₹100  
C) ₹200, ₹140, ₹87  
D) ₹210, ₹126, ₹91  

**Correct Answer: A) ₹196, ₹147, ₹84**

Let 3A = 4B = 7C = k.  
Then: A = k/3, B = k/4, C = k/7.

Sum of ratio terms = 28 + 21 + 12 = 61.  
A's share = (28/61) × 427 = ₹196. B's share = (21/61) × 427 = ₹147. C's share = (12/61) × 427 = ₹84.

## Question 11

**A sum of ₹12,500 amounts to ₹15,500 in 4 years at simple interest. What is the rate of interest?**

A) 3%  
B) 4%  
C) 5%  
D) 6%  

**Correct Answer: D) 6%**  
**Explanation:**  
Principal (P) = ₹12,500. Amount (A) = ₹15,500. SI = 15,500 − 12,500 = ₹3,000. Time (T) = 4 years.  
Using SI = (P × R × T) / 100: 3000 = (12500 × R × 4) / 100 ⇒ 3000 = 500R ⇒ R = 6%.

**Pipe A can fill a tank in 30 hours and Pipe B can fill it in 45 hours. If both the pipes are opened together, in how much time will the tank be filled?**

A) 15 hours  
B) 18 hours  
C) 20 hours  
D) 25 hours  

**Correct Answer: B) 18 hours**  
**Explanation:**

## Question 14

**A can do a certain job in 12 days. B is 60% more efficient than A. How many days will B alone take to complete the same job?**

A) 6 days  
B) 7 1/2 days  
C) 8 days  
D) 9 days  

**Correct Answer: B) 7 1/2 days**  
**Explanation:**  
B is 60% more efficient than A, so efficiency ratio A : B = 100 : 160 = 5 : 8.  
Since time is inversely proportional to efficiency, ratio of time A : B = 8 : 5.  
Let B take x days. Then 8 : 5 :: 12 : x ⇒ 8x = 60 ⇒ x = 7 1/2 days.

## Question 45

**The number of flip-flops required for a divide-by-12 circuit is:**

A) 16  
B) 12  
C) 6  
D) 4  

**Correct Answer: D) 4**  
**Explanation:**  
A divide-by-12 circuit must count 12 states. The number of states possible with n flip-flops is 2ⁿ.  
Since 2³ = 8 < 12 and 2⁴ = 16 > 12, 4 flip-flops are required.
