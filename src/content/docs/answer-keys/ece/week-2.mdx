## Question 1

1. The speed of a boat when travelling downstream is 32 km/hr, whereas when travelling upstream it is 28 km/hr. What is the speed of the boat in still water and the speed of the stream?

- A) 30 km/hr, 2 km/hr
- B) 28 km/hr, 4 km/hr
- C) 32 km/hr, 2 km/hr
- D) 30 km/hr, 4 km/hr

Correct Answer: A) 30 km/hr, 2 km/hr

Explanation:

Downstream speed = 32 km/hr

Upstream speed = 28 km/hr

Speed of boat in still water

= (32 + 28) / 2

= 60 / 2

= 30 km/hr

Speed of stream

= (32 − 28) / 2

= 4 / 2

= 2 km/hr

## Question 14

14. The value of k for which the quadratic equation kx(x − 2) + 6 = 0 has equal roots is:

- A) 2
- B) 3
- C) 6
- D) −6

Correct Answer: C) 6

Explanation:

Given equation

kx(x − 2) + 6 = 0

⇒ kx² − 2kx + 6 = 0

Comparing with ax² + bx + c = 0,

a = k

b = −2k

c = 6

For equal roots,

b² − 4ac = 0

⇒ (−2k)² − 4(k)(6) = 0

⇒ 4k² − 24k = 0

⇒ 4k(k − 6) = 0

⇒ k = 0 or k = 6

Since k ≠ 0 for a quadratic equation,

k = 6

## Question 15

15. Quadratic equation corresponding to the roots 2 + √5 and 2 − √5 is:

- A) x² − 4x − 1 = 0
- B) x² + 4x − 1 = 0
- C) x² − 2x − 1 = 0
- D) x² − 4x + 1 = 0

Correct Answer: A) x² − 4x − 1 = 0

Explanation:

Given roots:

α = 2 + √5

β = 2 − √5

Sum of roots

= α + β

= (2 + √5) + (2 − √5)

= 4

Product of roots

= αβ

= (2 + √5)(2 − √5)

= 4 − 5

= −1

Quadratic equation

= x² − (sum of roots)x + (product of roots)

= 0

⇒ x² − 4x − 1 = 0

18. An ARP Request is generally sent as:

- A) Unicast
- B) Multicast
- C) Point-to-point

## Question 30

30. Which statement is TRUE about blocking and non-blocking assignments?

- A) Both execute identically
- B) Blocking assignments execute in parallel
- C) Blocking assignments are mandatory in flip-flops
- D) Non-blocking assignments are mainly used in sequential logic

Correct Answer: D) Non-blocking assignments are mainly used in sequential logic

Explanation:

Blocking assignments $(=)$ execute statements sequentially in the order they appear.

Non-blocking assignments $(<=)$ schedule updates to occur simultaneously at the end of the current time step.

Because sequential circuits and flip-flops update their outputs together on clock edges, non-blocking assignments are preferred for modeling sequential logic.

- B) 45°

## Question 45

45. If transmission bandwidth is doubled in FM, SNR is

- A) doubled
- B) raised four times
- C) decreased four times
- D) halved

Correct Answer: B) raised four times

Explanation:

In Frequency Modulation (FM), the signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) is proportional to the square of the transmission bandwidth.

That is:

SNR ∝ BW²

If the bandwidth is doubled,

SNR ∝ (2BW)²

= 4BW²

Therefore, the signal-to-noise ratio becomes four times its original value.
