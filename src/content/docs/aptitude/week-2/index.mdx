Week 2 builds upon your foundational knowledge with more complex problems and advanced techniques.

## Week Overview

This week introduces intermediate concepts and bridges the gap between basic and advanced topics:

- **Quantitative Ability**: Percentages, profit/loss, and simple interest
- **Logical Reasoning**: Analogies and logical relationships
- **Verbal Reasoning**: Inference and critical reading
- **Data Interpretation**: Multi-data set analysis
- **Problem Solving**: Multi-step problem solving

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will:
- Apply percentage calculations to real-world scenarios
- Understand analogical relationships and patterns
- Make inferences from text
- Analyze complex data relationships
- Solve multi-layered problems

## This Week's Topics

- Quantitative Ability: Profit, Loss, and Discounts
- Logical Reasoning: Analogies and Word Relationships
- Verbal Reasoning: Making Inferences
- Data Interpretation: Combined Charts and Analysis
- Problem Solving: Working with Multiple Variables

## Key Formulas to Master

- Profit% = (SP-CP)/CP × 100
- Discount = M.P - S.P
- Simple Interest = P × R × T / 100

## Practice Strategy

- Complete at least 15 problems per subject
- Focus on accuracy over speed initially
- Review solutions and understand alternative approaches
- Identify your weak areas and practice more

Continue strengthening your aptitude foundation!
