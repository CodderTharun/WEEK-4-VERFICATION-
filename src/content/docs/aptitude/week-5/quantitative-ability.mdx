## Mixed Problem Examples

1. **Complex Scenario:**
A investor's portfolio: 40% in stocks (20% annual return), 30% in bonds (8% return), 30% in savings (3% return). Recalculate after all double, new allocation?

2. **Multi-step Problem:**
Factory producing items with efficiency improving 10% weekly. Week 1: 100 items. Inventory limited to 1000. When will production exceed 500 items/week?

3. **Integrated Problem:**
Loans: 60% of 1,00,000 at 12% per annum. Repay with profit 25% in 2 years. Monthly payment?
