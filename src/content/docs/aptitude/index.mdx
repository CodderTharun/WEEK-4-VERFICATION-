## Syllabus


| Week | Syllabus |
| --- | --- |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 1</strong></span> | Number System: Divisibility Rules, HCF & LCM, Factors and Multiples, Prime Numbers, Remainders, Cyclicity. Percentages: Percentage Basics, Increase & Decrease, Successive Percentages. Ratio & Proportion: Ratios, Proportion, Partnership, Variation. Averages: Basic Average, Weighted Average. Profit & Loss: Cost Price, Selling Price, Discount, Marked Price. Simple & Compound Interest: SI & CI Concepts and Applications. Time & Work: Work Efficiency, Pipes & Cisterns, Work and Wages. |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 2</strong></span> | Time, Speed & Distance: Relative Speed, Average Speed, Problems on Trains, Boats & Streams. Permutation & Combination: Fundamental Principle of Counting, Arrangements and Selections. Probability: Basic Probability Concepts and Applications. Algebra: Linear Equations, Quadratic Equations and Simplification. Geometry & Mensuration: Lines, Angles, Triangles, Circles, Areas, Volumes and Surface Areas. |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 3</strong></span> | Coding-Decoding. Blood Relations. Direction Sense. Seating Arrangement. Logical Puzzles. Series Problems (Number Series and Alphabet Series). |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 4</strong></span> | Time, Speed & Distance: Relative Speed, Average Speed, Problems on Trains, Boats & Streams. Permutation & Combination: Fundamental Principle of Counting, Arrangements and Selections. Probability: Basic Probability Concepts and Applications. Algebra: Linear and Quadratic Equations, Simplification Techniques. Geometry & Mensuration: Lines, Angles, Triangles, Circles, Areas, Volumes and Surface Areas. |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 5</strong></span> | Revision of Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning and Problem-Solving Concepts covered in previous weeks. |
