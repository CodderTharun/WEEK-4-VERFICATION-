# Aptitude - Week 1

Welcome to your first week of aptitude training! This week focuses on building strong fundamentals across key aptitude domains.

## Week Overview

This week covers the foundational concepts across all major aptitude areas:

- **Quantitative Ability**: Number systems and basic arithmetic
- **Logical Reasoning**: Pattern recognition and sequence analysis
- **Verbal Reasoning**: Reading comprehension and vocabulary
- **Data Interpretation**: Charts and graph fundamentals
- **Problem Solving**: Strategic approaches to complex problems

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, you will:
- Understand core numerical concepts and operations
- Recognize basic logical patterns
- Enhance your vocabulary and reading skills
- Interpret simple data visualizations
- Develop problem-solving strategies

## This Week's Topics

- Quantitative Ability: Number Systems
- Logical Reasoning: Series and Sequences
- Verbal Reasoning: Reading Comprehension Basics
- Data Interpretation: Bar Charts and Graphs
- Problem Solving: Breaking Down Complex Problems

## Practice Strategy

- Solve at least 10 problems per subject daily
- Review concepts before attempting problems
- Track your mistakes and review them weekly
- Start with easier problems to build confidence

Let's begin your aptitude preparation journey!
