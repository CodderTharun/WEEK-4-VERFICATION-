## Fluid Properties

### Density (ρ)
- Mass per unit volume
- kg/m³

### Viscosity (μ)
- Resistance to flow
- Pa·s

### Surface Tension (σ)
- Force at liquid surface
- N/m

## Fluid Behavior

- **Newtonian Fluids** - Constant viscosity (Water, Oil)
- **Non-Newtonian** - Viscosity changes (Blood, Ketchup)

## Static Pressure

P = ρgh

### Pascal's Principle
- Pressure transmits equally in all directions

### Hydrostatic Force
- F = ρghA_c
