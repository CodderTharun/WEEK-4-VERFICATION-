# Manufacturing - Week 1

## Manufacturing Processes

### Casting
- Pouring molten metal into mold
- Sand casting, die casting

### Forming
- Changing shape without removing material
- Forging, rolling, extrusion

### Machining
- Removing material
- Turning, milling, drilling

### Joining
- Connecting parts
- Welding, brazing, fastening

## Process Selection

Factors:
- Material properties
- Production volume
- Accuracy requirements
- Cost constraints
- Time constraints

## Quality Control

- Dimensional accuracy
- Surface finish
- Material properties verification
- Defect detection

### This Week

- Classification of processes
- Process characteristics
- Material removal rates
- Tool wear and life
- Process economics

### Practice

- Select appropriate processes
- Estimate production costs
- Design for manufacturability
