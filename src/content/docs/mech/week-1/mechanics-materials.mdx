# Mechanics of Materials - Week 1

## Stress

Force per unit area: σ = F/A

### Types
- **Normal Stress** - Perpendicular to area
- **Shear Stress** - Parallel to area (τ = F/A)
- **Tensile** - Pulling
- **Compressive** - Pushing

## Strain

Deformation per unit length: ε = ΔL/L₀

### Types
- **Normal Strain** - Change in length
- **Shear Strain** - Angular distortion

## Stress-Strain Relationship

- **Young's Modulus (E)**: E = σ/ε
- **Shear Modulus (G)**: G = τ/γ
- **Poisson's Ratio (ν)**: ν = -lateral strain/axial strain

## Material Properties

- Elastic limit
- Yield strength
- Ultimate strength
- Ductility and brittleness
