## Laws of Thermodynamics

### Zeroth Law
- Establishes thermal equilibrium concepts
- Foundation for temperature measurement

### First Law
- Conservation of energy
- ΔU = Q - W

### Second Law
- Entropy increases in isolated systems
- ΔS ≥ 0

### Third Law
- Entropy approaches zero at absolute zero

## Key Concepts

- **System** - Matter under study
- **Surroundings** - Everything outside system
- **Process** - Path system follows
- **State** - Condition of system (P, V, T)

## Work and Heat

- Work done by system: W = ∫PdV
- First law: ΔU = Q - W
