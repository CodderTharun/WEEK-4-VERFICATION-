## Fluid Kinematics

### Continuity Equation
ρ₁A₁V₁ = ρ₂A₂V₂

For incompressible: A₁V₁ = A₂V₂

## Bernoulli Equation

P/ρg + V²/2g + z = constant

### Applications
- Flow through pipes
- Nozzles
- Venturi meters
- Siphons

## Energy Equation

(P₁/ρg) + (V₁²/2g) + z₁ = (P₂/ρg) + (V₂²/2g) + z₂ + hf

### Head Loss
- Major losses: hf = f(L/D)(V²/2g)
- Minor losses: hm = K(V²/2g)
