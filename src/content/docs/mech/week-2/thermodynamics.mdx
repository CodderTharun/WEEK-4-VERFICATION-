## Thermodynamic Processes

### Isobaric (Constant Pressure)
- W = P(V₂ - V₁)
- Q = nCₚΔT

### Isochoric (Constant Volume)
- W = 0
- Q = nCᵥΔT

### Isothermal (Constant Temperature)
- W = nRT ln(V₂/V₁)
- ΔU = 0

### Adiabatic (No Heat Transfer)
- Q = 0
- W = -ΔU

## Power Cycles

### Otto Cycle

- Efficiency: η = 1 - 1/r^(γ-1)
