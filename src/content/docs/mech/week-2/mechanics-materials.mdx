# Mechanics of Materials - Week 2

## Bending

### Bending Stress
σ = My/I

Where:
- M = Bending moment
- y = Distance from neutral axis
- I = Moment of inertia

### Shear Stress in Beams
τ = VQ/(Ib)

## Deflection

### Slope and Deflection
- EI(d²y/dx²) = M(x)

### Common Formulas
- Cantilever: δ = PL³/3EI
- Simply supported: δ = 5wL⁴/384EI

## Torsion

### Torque and Angle
θ = TL/GJ

### Shear Stress
τ = TR/J
