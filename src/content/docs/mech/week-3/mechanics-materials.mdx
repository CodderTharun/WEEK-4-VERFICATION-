## Failure Criteria

### Static Failure
- Maximum normal stress theory
- Maximum shear stress theory
- von Mises theory

### Combined Loading
- Plane stress conditions
- Principal stresses: σ₁, σ₂

## Fatigue

### S-N Curve
- Relationship between stress and cycles

### Endurance Limit
- Maximum stress for infinite life
- Typically at 10⁶ cycles

### Factors Affecting Fatigue
- Surface finish
- Size
- Temperature
- Stress concentration

## Fracture Mechanics

- Stress intensity factor: K
- Fracture toughness
- Crack propagation
