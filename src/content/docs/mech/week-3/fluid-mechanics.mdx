## Pipe Flow

### Laminar Flow
- Re < 2300
- f = 64/Re

### Turbulent Flow
- Re > 4000
- Colebrook-White equation
- Moody diagram

### Friction Factor
- Dependent on Reynolds number
- Roughness of pipe

## Drag Force

### Drag Types
- Skin friction drag
- Pressure drag
- Form drag

### Drag Coefficient
- Cd = D/(½ρV²A)

### Terminal Velocity
- When drag equals weight
