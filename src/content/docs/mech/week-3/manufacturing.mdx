# Manufacturing - Week 3

## Casting Processes

### Sand Casting
- Green sand molds
- Advantages: Cost-effective, complex shapes
- Disadvantages: Surface finish, porosity

### Die Casting
- High-pressure injection
- Thin walls, tight tolerances
- Good surface finish

### Investment Casting
- Ceramic mold
- Complex shapes, precision

## Forming Processes

### Forging
- Hot and cold forging
- High strength
- Grain refinement

### Rolling
- Flat products
- High production rate
- Controlled thickness

### Extrusion
- Complex cross-sections
- Various materials

- Mold design

### Practice

- Design molds
