# Thermodynamics - Week 3

## Modes of Heat Transfer

### Conduction
- Heat through solid material
- Fourier's Law: q = -kA(dT/dx)

### Convection
- Heat with fluid motion
- h - Convection coefficient

### Radiation
- Heat transfer by EM waves
- Stefan-Boltzmann: q = σAT⁴

## Heat Exchangers

### Types
- Counter-flow
- Co-current
- Cross-flow
- Shell and tube

### Effectiveness
- NTU method
- LMTD method
