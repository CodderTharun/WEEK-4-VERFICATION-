# Mech

## Strength of Materials

| Week | Syllabus |
| --- | --- |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 1</strong></span> | Basic Engineering Definitions and Concepts. Stress-Strain Relations: Tension, Compression and Shear. Temperature Stresses and Indeterminate Structural Problems. Shear Force and Bending Moment Diagrams (SFD and BMD). |
