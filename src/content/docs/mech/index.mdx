## Strength of Materials

## Basic Thermodynamics

| Week | Syllabus |
| --- | --- |
| <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}><strong>Week 1</strong></span> | Basic Thermodynamics Engineering Definitions and Concepts. Macroscopic and Microscopic Approaches. Avogadro's Law and Dalton's Law of Partial Pressures. Vander Waal’s Equation and Compressibility Factor. |
