Welcome to your **5-Week Placement Preparation Notes**!

This app is organized by **departments** (IT, ECE, EEE) and structured across **5 weeks** with detailed notes for each subject.

## How to Navigate

- Select a **department** from the sidebar
- Choose a **week** to view the notes
- Each week contains detailed subject-wise information

## Departments Covered

- **IT** - Information Technology
- **ECE** - Electronics and Communication Engineering  
- **EEE** - Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Good luck with your placement preparation! 🚀
