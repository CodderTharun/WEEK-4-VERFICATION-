## Let's Get Placed - 2027

Welcome to **Let's Get Placed**, an initiative by the **Training and Placement Office, UVCE** to support students in their placement preparation journey.

This platform provides structured and regularly updated **subject-wise notes** tailored to the needs of students participating in the **LGP program**.

---

## Rules and Regulations

- All students are instructed to successfully complete the entire program.
- A minimum credit of **35%** should be maintained in each test.
- It is compulsory for students to attend all the tests.
- There will be **no negative marking**.

---

## General Schedule

The general schedule for the program will be as follows:

- Notes for the respective subjects will be provided every **Monday**.
- Tests for the same will be conducted the following **Saturday and Sunday**.

---

## Important Note

It is advised to not completely rely on the notes provided.

Stay focused and make the best use of the resources provided.

> Wishing you all the best in your placement journey!!!
