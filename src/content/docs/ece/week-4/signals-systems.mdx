## Filter Types

### Low-pass Filter

- Attenuates high frequencies
- Passes low frequencies
- Cutoff frequency fc

### High-pass Filter

- Attenuates low frequencies
- Passes high frequencies

### Band-pass Filter

- Passes frequencies in band
- Attenuates outside band

### Band-stop Filter

- Blocks frequencies in band
- Passes outside band

## Filter Design

### Butterworth Filter

- Maximally flat passband
- Smooth roll-off

### Chebyshev Filter

- Steeper roll-off
- Ripple in passband

### Bessel Filter

- Maximally flat group delay
- Better phase characteristics
