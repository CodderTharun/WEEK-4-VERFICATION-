# Power Electronics - Week 4

## Switching Control

### Controlled Rectifiers

- Diode rectifier (uncontrolled)
- Thyristor rectifier (controlled)
- Firing angle control

### Thyristor (SCR)

- Gate-triggered on
- Turns off at zero crossing
- Used in AC control

## Resonant Converters

- Zero voltage switching (ZVS)
- Zero current switching (ZCS)
- Reduced switching losses

### Advantages

- Lower EMI
- Lower switching losses
- Better efficiency
