## Counters

Sequential circuits counting pulses.

### Types

- Ripple Counter (asynchronous)
- Synchronous Counter
- Up/Down Counters
- Programmable Counters

### Counter Modulo

- MOD-n counter: Counts 0 to n-1
- MOD-10 (decade counter)
- MOD-16 (4-bit counter)

## Registers

Storage elements.

### Types

- Storage registers
- Shift registers
- Buffer registers

### Shift Register Operations

- Serial In, Serial Out (SISO)
- Serial In, Parallel Out (SIPO)
- Parallel In, Serial Out (PISO)
- Parallel In, Parallel Out (PIPO)
