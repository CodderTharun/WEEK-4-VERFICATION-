## Signal Classification

### Continuous vs Discrete

- **Continuous** - Defined for all time
- **Discrete** - Defined at specific instants

### Periodic vs Aperiodic

- **Periodic** - Repeats with period T
- **Aperiodic** - Non-repeating

### Even and Odd Signals

- **Even** - x(-t) = x(t)
- **Odd** - x(-t) = -x(t)

## System Properties

### Linear vs Nonlinear

- **Linear** - Satisfies superposition
- **Nonlinear** - Doesn't satisfy superposition

### Time-Invariant vs Time-Variant

- **Time-Invariant** - Properties don't change with time
- **Time-Variant** - Properties change with time
