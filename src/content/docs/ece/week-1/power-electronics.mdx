## Power Devices

Semiconductors for power conversion.

### Diodes

- Forward conducting at ~0.7V
- Reverse blocking
- Used in rectification

### Transistors

1. **BJT (Bipolar Junction Transistor)**
   - Controlled by base current
   - NPN and PNP types

2. **MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor FET)**
   - Controlled by gate voltage
   - N-channel and P-channel

### Characteristics

- Voltage ratings
- Current ratings
- Power dissipation
- Switching speed

## Rectification

Converting AC to DC.

### Half-Wave Rectifier

- Conducts half cycle
- Simple but inefficient

### Full-Wave Rectifier

- Conducts full cycle
- Better output
