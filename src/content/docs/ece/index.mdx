# ECE Syllabus
 
---
 
 
## C Programming

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
| <span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 1**</span> | Variables and Data Types. Operators and Expressions. Conditional Statements (`if`, `switch`). Loops (`for`, `while`, `do-while`). Functions and Recursion. Arrays and Strings. Pointers. Structures and Unions. Bitwise Operators. File Handling. Embedded C. |

---

## Network Theory

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
|<span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 1**</span> | Mesh Analysis. Nodal Analysis. KCL and KVL. Thevenin's Theorem. Norton's Theorem. Maximum Power Transfer Theorem. Two-Port Networks (XY and ABCD Parameters). Superposition Method. |

---

## Digital Electronics

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
| <span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 1**</span> | Introduction to Digital Electronics. Boolean Algebra & Logic: Rules, SOP & POS Conversion, Logic Gates. Number Systems & Conversions: Binary, Octal, Decimal, Hexadecimal, Arithmetic & Complements, Data Representation & Codes. K-Map Simplification. Combinational Circuits: Parity Generator, Adders, Subtractors, Multiplexer, Demultiplexer, Comparator, Encoder and Decoder. Sequential Circuits: Latches, Flip Flops, Counters and Shift Registers. Implementation of Complex Sequential Circuits. Verilog. |

---

## Analog Electronics

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
| <span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 2**</span> | Diodes, Transistors and IV Characteristics and Operations. Clipping and Clamping Circuits, Rectifiers and Other Electronic Circuits. Oscillators. Positive and Negative Feedback Concepts. Virtual Ground and Bandwidth Concepts. Frequency Domain Analysis. Active Filters. Amplifiers: Op-Amp, Class A, B, AB and Unit Gain Amplifiers. Applications of Op-Amps: Schmitt Trigger, 555 Timer. Two-Port Networks. |

---

## Analog and Digital Communication

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
| <span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 2**</span> | Analog Modulation: AM, Spectrum, Power, Modulation & Detection. AM Systems: DSB-SC, SSB, Ring Modulator and Coherent Detection. Noise Concepts: Thermal Noise, Shot Noise, SNR and Noise Figure. Angle Modulation: FM, PM, NBFM, WBFM, Bandwidth and Demodulation. Noise in Receivers: AM/FM Receiver Noise, Pre-emphasis and De-emphasis. Digital Communication: Sampling, PAM, TDM, PCM and Quantization. Baseband Transmission: Line Coding, ISI, Eye Pattern and Equalization. Digital Modulation: BPSK, QPSK, DPSK, BFSK, M-PSK and QAM. |

---

## Computer Networks (IoT)

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
| <span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 2**</span> | TCP/IP Model. Five Layers of TCP/IP Model. |

## VLSI

| Week | Syllabus |
|---|---|
| <span style="white-space: nowrap;">**Week 3**</span> | Digital VLSI: Basics of MOSFETs, Operation, I-V Characterization and Second Order Effects. Static Characteristics. Combinational MOS Circuits (NAND2, NOR2, Transmission Logic Circuits). Sequential MOS Circuits (Latches and Bistable Elements). Dynamic Logic. Timing Issues. Semiconductor Memory. Analog VLSI: Single Stage Amplifiers, Differential Amplifiers, Operational Amplifiers, Capacitor Circuits, Oscillators and PLLs. |

---

| Week | Syllabus |
