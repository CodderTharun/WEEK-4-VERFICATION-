# Power Electronics - Week 2

## DC-DC Converters

Converting one DC voltage to another.

### Buck Converter

- Voltage step-down
- Lower output voltage
- PWM controlled

### Boost Converter

- Voltage step-up
- Higher output voltage  
- PWM controlled

### Buck-Boost Converter

- Can step-up or down
- Inverted polarity

## Inverters

Converting DC to AC.

### Half-Bridge Inverter

- Simple two-switch design
- Output has DC offset

### Full-Bridge Inverter

- Four-switch design
- Pure AC output
- Better efficiency
