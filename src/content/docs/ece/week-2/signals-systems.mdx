## Fourier Series

Representing periodic signals as sum of sinusoids.

### Frequency Domain

- Fundamental frequency f₀
- Harmonics at nf₀
- Magnitude and phase spectrum

### Fourier Coefficients

- a₀ - DC component
- aₙ, bₙ - Fourier coefficients
- Parseval's theorem

## Fourier Transform

Extending Fourier series to aperiodic signals.

### Properties

- Linearity
- Time shift
- Frequency shift
- Scaling

- Fourier transform properties

- Frequency spectrum analysis
- Applications in filtering
