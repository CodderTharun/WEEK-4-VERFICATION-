# Signals & Systems - Week 5

## Sampling

Converting continuous to discrete signals.

### Sampling Theorem

fs ≥ 2 × fmax

### Aliasing

Occurs when fs < 2 × fmax
Causes frequency ambiguity

### Quantization

Converting continuous amplitude to discrete levels.
